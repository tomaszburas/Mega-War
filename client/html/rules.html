Registration

  1. Username must be longer than 3 characters.
  2. Password must contains minimum 5 characters, at least one letter and one number.

Profile

  1. You can save changes to the configuration of warrior skills after 3 hours have passed since the last change.

Battle

  1. At the beginning, everyone has as many health points (HP) as their toughness * 10. Everyone has as many shields (DP) as their defense value.
  2. The warrior that starts makes an attack equal to his strength.
  3. If the attacked warrior has shield + agility greater than attack power, then:
    1. A shield in the amount of attack is taken from him.
      1. If the attack was greater than the shield, the remaining amount of attack strength is deducted from its health.
      2. If the attack was up to the current target, no life is deducted.
    2. If condition 3 is not met, we simply subtract the attack from life.
  4. The order is changed and the attacker is now attacked and the attacker is attacked.
  5. Repeat points 2 - 4 until someone dies, i.e. his HP drops to min. 0. When one of the fighters dies, the attacker is the winner.
  6. You can start a new battle after 60 seconds have elapsed since the last battle.
  7. You can start a new battle with the same user after 1 hour has elapsed since the last battle.
